About Healthy food checker Application


This application is all about getting nutritional contents of the food or drinks captured at the time of consumption.
In order to get these details, User need to first sign up/sign in to the app using his registered e-mail/mobile number and then should be able to capture the food or drinks.
The Estimated quantity will be shown using augmented view once the user takes a picture of food or drinks.Based on the picture captured, App will display all the nutritional content (including protein, carbs, fats, fiber,vitamins, etc.) of the foods or drinks captured by user.
App also calculates daily calorie count (including protein, carbs,fats, fiber, vitamins, etc.) of all the foods recorded as consumed by the user.
